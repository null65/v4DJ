MeshCore Setup

By my friends at VARC I was introduced to a platform called meshcore, this allows for basic text-messaging over the protocol LoRa - (Or Long range). So I bought myself a Heltec V3 which is one of the most common and best starter boards for 868mhz.

UPDATE: Kindly gifted by Matt - G7HMV A second Heltec V3 and a Vertical High gain antenna, Which is soon to be installed on my roof!
